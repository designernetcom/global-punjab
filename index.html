About Us
Perfect place for special events

We created a sophisticated, warm, and harmonious setting to reflect the warmth of India’s hospitality when we opened this trend-setting restaurant in January 2013. After making a culinary mark in the heart of Magarpatta City, we spread our love for hospitality to Kharadi in 2014 near the iconic World Trade Centre and EON IT Park. In 2016, we added a jewel to the crown of our chain - Global Punjab, Nanded City, which is our the biggest restaurant spread across 9000 sq ft. situated near Sinhagad Road, Pune. Global Punjab at Wakad was launched in 2018 and is one of the most loved family restaurants in the area. In 2021 our latest and 5th installment of the brand GP is our newest outlet at Nulstop (Kothrud) which is also our first franchise outlet and is already a buzz in the area.
