About Restaurant
Think Global
Eat Punjab!

We created a sophisticated, warm, and harmonious setting to reflect the warmth of India’s hospitality when we opened this trend-setting restaurant in Magarpatta City, Hadapsar (Pune) in January 2013. We named our venture 'Global Punjab' to showcase the fusion between good quality food and world-class service. Our passion for serving people is what drove us from the corporate world in the hospitality industry.

At Global Punjab, we offer you a wide range of dishes featuring exquisite spices and unusual ingredients prepared by our team of expert, dedicated chefs who have been in our team for the last 9 years. Some signature dishes that have wowed our patrons are : Tangdi Mumtaz, Kalimiri Tangdi Kabab, Ajwaini Paneer, Tandoori Stuffed Mushrooms, GP Special Butter Chicken, Dal Makhani, Veg Patiala, and Murg Mussallam. We aim at making dining at Global Punjab is a wholesome experience in terms of the ambiance, service, entertainment, and of course, delicious food! Ghazal singers, private parties, and live music are just some of the contributions to this experience.

After making a culinary mark in the heart of Magarpatta City since 2013, we spread our love for hospitality to Kharadi 2014 near the iconic World Trade Centre and EON IT Park. In 2016, we added a jewel to the crown of our chain - Global Punjab, Nanded City, which is our biggest restaurant spread across 9000 sq ft. situated near Sinhagad Road, Pune. Global Punjab at Wakad was launched in 2018 and is one of the most loved family restaurants in the area. In 2021 our latest and 5th installment of the brand GP is our newest outlet at Nulstop (Kothrud) which is also our first franchise outlet and is already a buzz in the area.

All our outlets are controlled and monitored and supplied from our head office and central kitchen with our central store situated in Kharadi was created in 2019 to support quality, consistency, and overall control and meet the demand for expansion of this beloved growing chain of North Indian restaurants in Pune. We have our fleet and support our restaurants with our own logistics support.

We warmly invite you to take this culinary journey with us, to savor and discover new ingredients and dishes. We look forward to serving you again and again and again.
